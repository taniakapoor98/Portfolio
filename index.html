  • Sleek and interactive GUI for effortless user experience.
  • Visual workload categorization: Low, Medium, High.
  • Ideal for cognitive neuroscience, HCI, and mental health tracking.

SurplusPredict AI – The Future of Renewable Energy Forecasting

GitHub: View on GitHub

Technical Details: Built with Python, LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) networks, leveraging time-series analysis.

Features:

  • Accurate 24-hour surplus energy predictions for solar and wind energy.
  • Optimized to minimize false positives and increase prediction reliability.
  • Built with LSTM models for recognizing complex, recurring patterns in time-series data.
  • Solar energy model peaks in April, June, and July, with 80%+ prediction accuracy.
  • Surplus energy calculated based on threshold values, ensuring smarter energy distribution.

SentimentMaster AI – Sentiment Analysis Solution

GitHub: View on GitHub

Technical Details: Built with Python, VADER sentiment analysis, PCA, KMeans clustering, DistilBERT (fine-tuned with PyTorch)

Features:

  • Unsupervised model with VADER, PCA, and KMeans for classifying customer sentiment.
  • Fine-tuned DistilBERT transformer model for high-performance discriminative analysis, achieving a 0.91 F1 score.
  • Rule-based system combining both models to boost accuracy and reliability.
  • Benchmarked against GPT-3.5-Turbo for cutting-edge insights into Generative AI’s effectiveness.
  • Comprehensive analysis of customer emotions to drive smarter business decisions.
